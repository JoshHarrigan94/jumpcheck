What these metrics mean
RSI proxy

RSI proxy estimates how much flight height you produce relative to ground contact time. Higher usually suggests better reactive stiffness, but only when contact detection is reliable.

Force proxy

Force proxy is estimated from body mass and phone acceleration. It is not a force plate measurement, but can be useful for comparing trends under consistent setup conditions.

Rotation load

Rotation load uses gyroscope movement to estimate how much twisting, wobble, or stabilisation demand occurred during the test.

Exposure score

Exposure score combines contacts, jump intensity, landing force, direction, and stability into a practical training load estimate.
